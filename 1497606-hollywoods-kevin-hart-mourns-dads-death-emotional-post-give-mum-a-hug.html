Hollywood's Kevin Hart Mourns His Dad's Death in Emotional Post: &quot;Give Mum a Hug&quot;

 Posted on August 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  482 words  |  Billy Koelling
  • Stand-up comedian Kevin Hart announced the death of his father, Henry Robert Witherspoon online
  • The father of four appreciated his dad for making him a better father and shared heartwarming photos with him
  • Hart's fans flocked to the comment section as they sent their heartfelt tributes to encourage the superstar

It is a heartbreaking moment for American comedian Kevin Darnell Hart following the demise of his father, Henry Robert Witherspoon.

Hart's message to late dad

The American actor announced the death of his dad on social media, as he mourned the huge loss.

Hart penned an emotional message online, pouring his heart out after the saddening news.

"RIP to one of the realest and rawest to ever do it…Love you, dad. Gone but never forgotten….Give mum a hug for me….y’all did good, man. Thank you for everything….I’m a better father because of you We will all make you proud," he painfully wrote.
