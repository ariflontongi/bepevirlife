  • A Nigerian man who describes himself as a content creator made people laugh during a visit to his niece
  • Announcing his presence as the kid's uncle, the man threw N50 biscuits to the girl who was seated
  • Many Nigerians who watched his video said they had finally seen an affordable TikTok trend they could join

A young Nigerian man has made many people laugh as he shared a video of his niece on TiTok.

The man (@zubby2f_) walked into a room with the kid sitting on a couch. He approached the girl and said:

"Uncle is here."

Uncle buys biscuits for his niece

He opened his handbag and started "spraying" the kid N50 biscuits. He threw her the snacks one after the other as if they were money.
