It is a season of proposal, engagement, and weddings in the Nigerian gospel music industry, as singer Neon Adejo has proposed to his partner Lade Kehinde, a doctor.

Neon Adejo shared the good news on Friday, March 15, 2024, disclosing that he has found his "good thing."

The gospel singer known for his hit song 'Eze Ebube' shared pictures and a video from the proposal in London, the UK, showing the moment he took Kehinde to a beautifully designed rooftop before going down on his knee to pop the question.
