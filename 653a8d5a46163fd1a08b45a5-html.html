Ana Maria Markovic challenges Alisha Lehmann for title of world's sexiest female footballer

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  286 words  |  Billy Koelling

There is a new debate in the world of football, this time to determine who is the sexiest female footballer on the planet.

Some believe it is Ana Maria Markovic, the 23-year-old Croatian who mixes her talent in playing for Grasshopper with her beauty and business as an influencer.

She was recently asked on social media if she agrees that she is the most beautiful player in women's football and she quote-tweeted that question.

"For you to decide," was her sole comment, along with a couple of emojis.

Who is Ana Maria Markovic?

Ana Maria Markovic was born in Split, Croatia, on November 9, 1999, and she currently plays in the Swiss Women's Super League with Grasshopper.

She is also a regular for the Croatian women's national team, while the footballer has become a celebrity outside of football.

She is very popular on social media, with 2.9 million followers on her Instagram account alone, where she shares her day-to-day life, as well as some personal messages.

Ana Maria Markovic vs Alisha Lehmann

If Ana Maria Markovic is to earn the title of being the sexiest female footballer around, she'll have to dethrone Alisha Lehmann.

The Swiss striker is one of the most famous female footballers on the world scene thanks to the number of followers she has on her Instagram account.

Currently playing for Aston Villa, she is the player with the most followers on her social media profiles, boasting nine and a half million followers on TikTok and more than 14 million on her Instagram.

Those numbers cement Alisha Lehmann as the most popular and sexiest female footballer for now, but she may soon face a challenge.
