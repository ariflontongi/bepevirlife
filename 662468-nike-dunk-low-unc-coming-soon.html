Nike Dunk Low &quot;UNC&quot; Coming Soon

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  546 words  |  Kelle Repass

If you pay attention to the sneaker world, you know that the Nike Dunk Low has been getting a whole lot of shine. Ever since 2020, the sneaker has seen a huge resurgence. Although it never "died," it wasn't exactly on trend during the whole dad shoe crazy. However, it is back and better than ever. This has subsequently led to a whole lot of colorways that fans are always eager to cop. Some might say the shoe is overstaying its welcome, however, others are happy to see it thriving, once again.

Throughout 2023, we have seen this shoe truly do numbers. A lot of this has to do with the fact that Jumpman is constantly bringing out new colorways. Almost every single day, we are bringing you the latest news on what is making its way to the market. New offerings are perpetually being unveiled, and this is great news for consumers. Simply put, they have more choices than they know what to do with. Now, we have a new Nike Dunk Low to show off, this time called "UNC" for short.

"UNC" Nike Dunk Low

Image via Nike

Upon looking at this sneaker, you can immediately understand why the whole "UNC" nickname is being given here. Firstly, this Nike Dunk Low begins with a white leather base. Secondly, the shoe gets some nice navy blue overlays. This is then followed up by University Blue on the Nike swoosh. When you combine these colors, you get something that could definitely have been worn by Michael Jordan in the 80s. If you love this color combination, then these will certainly be a must-cop.

For now, a release date for these is unknown. However, you will be able to cop them for a price of $110 USD. Hopefully, we get to see even more great Nike Dunk Low offerings, very soon. We will always keep you informed of the biggest releases from the biggest brands.
