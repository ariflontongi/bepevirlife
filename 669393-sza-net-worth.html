The Rise Of SZA

CHICAGO, IL - JULY 18: SZA performs during 2014 Pitchfork Music Festival at Union Park on July 18, 2014 in Chicago, Illinois. (Photo by Barry Brecheisen/WireImage)

SZA's journey to stardom began with the release of her debut EP, See.SZA.Run, in 2012. The EP showcased her unique voice and songwriting ability, quickly catching the attention of the music industry. In 2013, she signed with Top Dawg Entertainment, the record label behind notable artists like Kendrick Lamar and Schoolboy Q. This partnership paved the way for her critically acclaimed debut album, Ctrl, in 2017.

Ctrl Success & Accolades

The release of Ctrl solidified SZA's place in the music world. The album received widespread praise from both critics and fans. It earned her multiple Grammy nominations, including Best Urban Contemporary Album and Best New Artist. Ctrl went on to achieve Platinum status, selling over 1 million copies in the United States. This massive success significantly contributed to SZA's net worth, boosting her earning potential through album sales, streaming royalties, and live performances.
