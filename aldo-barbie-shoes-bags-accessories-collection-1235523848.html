Restock Date for Heels, Bags, Accessories The Hollywood Reporter

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  569 words  |  Tandra Barner

If the past year and a half of Barbiecore isn’t any indication, it’s Greta Gerwig’s blockbuster Barbie world, and we’re just living in it. In June, Aldo and the Mattel-owned doll brand released a range of playful pink accessories that quickly sold out — and those who missed out will want to mark set an iCal reminder for Thursday, Sept. 14, when the Dream Closet wardrobe staples are restocked.

Related: The Best Barbie Beauty Collections, From Bright Pink Hair Tools to Under-$15 Makeup

According to the accessories company’s website, the limited-edition Aldo x Barbie collection ($30-$140) will be available again online on Sept. 14. The collab comprises ’90s- and Y2K-inspired footwear, bags and jewelry in powder pink, bold fuschia and iridescent rosé hues. Shoes include sneakers, kitten heel mules, translucent slingbacks, four-inch stilettos, stacked heart pumps and chunky platform sandals, all featuring Aldo’s Pillow Walk insoles made of dual-density cushioned foam or bouncy lightweight cushioning for comfort no matter the height. Though most of the collection is sold out on Aldo’s website, you can still select pieces from the Barbie collab on Dillards.com, Nordstrom.com, Macys.com and Zappos.com.

For carryall collectors, there are microbags with allover Barbie logos, a bedazzled baguette bag and a quilted crossbody bag with chain straps. Barbie fans can finish their looks with heart-shaped earrings, chunky silver chain pendant necklaces and oversized gemstone rings.

Related: Bloomingdale’s and Barbie‘s New Movie-Inspired Collection to Pop Up in NY and LA

The collection first debuted ahead of the Barbie movie starring Margot Robbie as Barbie and Ryan Gosling as Ken. The box-office-record-breaking film follows the iconic pink-loving protagonist as she faces an existential crisis and leaves Barbie Land (in her pink Chevrolet Corvette convertible, no less!) for the Real World. Co-written by Gerwig and partner Noah Baumbach, the romantic comedy also stars Kate McKinnon, Dua Lipa, Issa Rae, Ritu Arya, Michael Cera and Emerald Fennell as Mattel dolls in the flesh and America Ferrera, Helen Mirren, Will Ferrell and others as humans. (Movie tickets are now available online through Fandango.)
