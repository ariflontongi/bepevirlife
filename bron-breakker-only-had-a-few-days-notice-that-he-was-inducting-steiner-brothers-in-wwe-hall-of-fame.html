Bron Breakker Only Had A Few Days Notice That He Was Inducting Steiner Brothers In WWE Hall Of Fame

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  239 words  |  Martina Birk

Bron Breakker has been on a roll after his debut in NXT 2.0 back in September last year. He has been booked very well as the NXT Champion as well. Obviously, he is just getting started.

The current NXT Champion has defeated everyone on his path and continues to be one of the biggest stars in NXT. Not only that, but he is also dating Cora Jade, which fans approve of.

Breakker also honored the Steiner Brothers during their WWE Hall Of Fame ceremony. While speaking during “The Black Announce Table“, Bron Breakker revealed he was also informed in advance he was going to be part of the ceremony.

“I actually never got a phone call. Nobody told me like clear-cut, ‘You’re doing it.’

I heard that they announced them and I knew that I was going to be a part of that in some form or fashion and I just kind of assumed that I was going to be the one to induct them, but they didn’t really tell me until like a couple days before — a day before — I can’t really remember, but I just remember not knowing until we were kind of going.”

Bron Breakker successfully defended his NXT Championship at NXT Halloween Havoc. We will have to wait and see what is next in store for the young star.
