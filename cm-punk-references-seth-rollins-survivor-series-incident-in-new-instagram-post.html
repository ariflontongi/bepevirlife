CM Punk References Seth Rollins Survivor Series Incident in New Instagram Post

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  197 words  |  Tandra Barner

CM Punk’s return at the Survivor Series Premium Live Event sent shockwaves across the world of professional wrestling thanks to its sheer electricity. During his brief appearance at the event, fans noticed that Seth Rollins was visibly furious seeing Punk. Now Punk has referenced that whole incident with an amusing fanart.

As Punk made his way out of the entranceway, the WWE universe couldn’t believe their eyes. That being said, Seth Rollins had an explosive reaction to Punk’s reappearance. Rollins was visibly irate, flipping the bird at Punk and even exclaiming ‘F*** you.’ 

The Second City Saint is clearly well aware of what fans have been thinking of Seth Rollins’ outburst, as he took to his Instagram story and referenced the incident by sharing a cartoon image of the ordeal.

CM Punk’s first major feud will be with familiar foe. Ringside News exclusively reported what brand CM Punk will be part of as well. We’ll have to wait and see how Punk will be booked in the coming weeks.
