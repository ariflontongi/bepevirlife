Courteney Cox Holds Hands with Look-Alike Daughter at 'Scream VI' Premiere

 Posted on August 18, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  359 words  |  Tandra Barner

Courteney Cox and daughter Coco Arquette had a sweet family moment on the red carpet.

Yesterday, the two attended the premiere of Scream VI in New York City and walked the carpet in contrasting elegant looks.

Photos from the event at the AMC Lincoln Square Theater show Cox looking cool in a little black dress and silky blazer of the same length. The Friends star wore the LBD with sheer black tights and pointed-toe heels. Ever the minimalist, she accessorized only with a black leather bag with a golden chain shoulder strap, and a silver chain necklace.

She also wore her hair down in messy waves. And her makeup look was bronzy, with a dark eye, pink blush, and a pink glossy lip.

Nina Westervelt//Getty Images

Next to her, her 18-year-old daughter looked so glamorous in a red cocktail dress. The piece featured thick straps and a ruched neckline with a string tie in the middle. The flared skirt fell just above her ankles, where a pair of black platform sandals with ankle straps took the spotlight.

Arquette kept her glam simple, opting for a sleek blowout that softly framed her face, a black cat eye, pink blush, and a pink nude lip.

Nina Westervelt//Getty Images

Arquette and Cox held hands as they happily posed for photos.

Cox shares the teen with ex-husband David Arquette. The two married in 1999, while she was filming Friends, and divorced in 2013. The actress has not remarried since, nor welcomed any more children, but she has been dating singer Johnny McDaid since her split from her ex.

McDaid and Cox started their romance in 2013 and got engaged after nine months together. They called off their engagement in late 2015, but reconciled in 2016, and have been going strong since.
