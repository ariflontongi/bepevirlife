Gabi DeMartino (born May 5, 1995, age: 27 years) is a popular singer, actress, and Youtuber from America. She is famous for being the twin sister of Niki DeMartino. Both sisters are popular among the fans for their partnered youtube channels Niki and Gabi. Gabi along with her sisters entertains the audience with their songs, v blogs, and other videos.

Gabi also runs her personal youtube channel titled “fancy vlogs by gab” where she is having 3.15 million subscribers. On her channel, she does not share her singing video but shares her food, shopping, home, makeup, and other videos.

About Gabi DeMartino

Gabi DeMartino is a versatile artist who has worked in her own reality web series with her sister Niki by Awesomeness TV. This web series has telecasted 5 episodes to date. As a singer, Gabi released her album “Paintings of Me” on May 6, 2022. Working along with her twin sister Niki she released their album Individual for which they both won Streamy Award in 2018.
