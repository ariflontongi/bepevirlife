Along with designing footwear for Sperry, EGOT musician and activist John Legend is also a skincare entrepreneur with his company, Loved01 — and you can now get the goodies delivered in as soon as one day, thanks to the brand’s launch on Amazon Beauty.

The 45-year-old star with dermatologist Dr. Naana Boakye to debut Loved01 in January 2023, with a goal of affordable, unisex skin-care formulated especially for melanin-rich skin. Designed for different skin types and tones, the brand harnesses the power of natural ingredients traditionally used in communities of color, such as nourishing shea butter (which traces its roots back to West Africa), moisturizing jojoba and coconut oils and inflammation-reducing rosehip and sea buckthorn oils. Prices range from $10 for calming facial wipes and $15 for exfoliating cleansers to $24 for a two-pack of face and body shaving cream.
