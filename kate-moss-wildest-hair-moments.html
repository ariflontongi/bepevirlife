Dear Kate, we were not prepared.

The supermodel surprised us all when she turned it up for Marc Jacobs new campaign, rocking vibrant pink hair. Could it be another fashionable nod to Barbiecore… or was Kate just reviving the iconic 1990s style she championed at Versace’s spring/summer 1999 show?

Although we have come to know Kate as one of the UK’s most enigmatic and recognisable blondes, normally rocking her signature messy rock-star girlfriend barnett… in reality the supermodel has not shied away from experimenting with her hair. 

From pixie crops to fringes we track Kate Moss’ most wild hair transformations of all time: 

Kate Moss’ Pixie Crop

Kate in 2001

Her best friend James Brown was enlisted to chop her hair into a gamine pixie cut in the early 2000s, sported here at the "Artificial Intelligence: A.I." World Premiere in 2001. Kate embraced the empowerment-shrouded style made popular by Audrey Hepburn 50 years prior.

Kate Moss’ Pink Hair 

Kate in 1998

Jeurgen teller’s famous photo was the first time we saw Kate rock pink hair. The shot called ‘Young Pink Kate’ taken in 1998 was created after Moss agreed to dye her hair to walk in Versace's spring/summer 1999 show. Coloured by Laurie Foley. Iconic.

Kate Moss' Shaggy Bob

Kate in 2008

In 2008 The Guardian said of Kate’s style “The messy bob has become the must-have hairdo of the season” She wore her trailblazing tresses to the Agent Provocateur fragrance launch party at the Dolce Club on September 25, 2008 in London.

Kate Moss' Brunette 

Kate in 2000

For the Vogue 1999 fashion awards Kate went brunette, turning in the new century with her darkest shade to date. She matched her hair to her now-vintage-trending leather jacket for the VH1 Fashion Awards in New York.

Kate Moss' Fringe

Kate in 1995

And again in 2010

Kate Moss has rocked a blunt fringe twice in her 35 year career. The first instance in the mid 90s during her relationship with Johnny Depp, and the second almost 10 years later. James Brown told Glamour magazine “She cut the fringe she had in 2007 herself, copying a picture of Marianne Faithfull”.
