What colors does coolant come in? Variations of coolant/antifreeze can come in; pink, red, orange, blue, green, and yellow. This can make it confusing when trying to decide which coolant you need in your vehicle. The different colors can mean different properties of the liquid. Is it OK to mix different color coolants? It's never a good idea to mix two different colors or types of antifreeze.
