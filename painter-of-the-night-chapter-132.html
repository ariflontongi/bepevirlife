Raw Scan Release Date

Raw scans are the original, unedited, and untranslated images of the manhwa that are leaked online before the official release. Some fans like to read the raw scans to get a glimpse of the new chapter ahead of time, even if they don’t understand the language.

However, reading the raw scans is not recommended, because they are illegal, low-quality, and inaccurate. They also harm the author and the publisher, who rely on the official sales and views to continue their work. Therefore, we urge you to support the official release and avoid the raw scans.

That being said, if you are still interested in the raw scans, they are usually released a few hours before the official release, depending on the source and the availability. That means that the raw scans of Chapter 132 might be available on November 28, 2023, in the morning or afternoon KST.

Recap of Previous Chapter 131

In case you need a quick recap of what happened in the previous chapter, here is a brief summary:

  • Chapter 131 was a flashback of Seungho’s past, where he was a young and rebellious nobleman who hated his family and the society he lived in.
  • He met a painter named Yoon Seungha, who was his cousin Yoon Bumjin’s friend and tutor. Seungho was instantly drawn to Seungha’s beauty and talent, and he decided to hire him as his personal painter.
  • Seungho and Seungha developed a close bond, and they soon realized that they had feelings for each other. They began a secret affair, knowing that it was dangerous and forbidden.
  • Seungho’s father found out about their relationship and tried to separate them. He arranged a marriage for Seungho with a woman from another noble family, and he threatened to kill Seungha if he ever saw him again.
  • Seungho refused to marry the woman and ran away with Seungha. They lived happily together for a while, until they were attacked by Seungho’s father’s men, who set their house on fire.
