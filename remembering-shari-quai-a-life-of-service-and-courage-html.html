Remembering Shari Quai: A Life of Service and Courage

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  562 words  |  Patria Henriques

In this article, we pay tribute to the remarkable life of Shari Quai, a dedicated activist and community leader whose legacy continues to inspire us all. Join us as we delve into her journey, the cause of her demise, and the indelible mark she left on the world. Shari Quai was a cherished figure in both the Portland and Draper communities, renowned for her unwavering commitment to cancer research and her tireless efforts to assist those in need. Her story is one of resilience, compassion, and the relentless pursuit of a better world.

A Passionate Advocate

Shari Quai’s journey in the medical field was a testament to her dedication. After accumulating 14 years of experience as an aesthetic injector, she took a courageous step and opened her own medical and wellness spa in Draper, Utah. Her expertise extended to various areas, including Dysport dermal fillers, bio-stimulant fillers, PRF, PDO threads, and skincare. Additionally, since 2014, she had been practicing general anesthesia as a CRNA-APRN at Jordan Valley and West Valley hospitals in West Jordan, Utah.

A Courageous Battle

Regrettably, on Thursday, April 27th, 2023, Shari Quai succumbed to the complications arising from her battle with cancer. Despite facing this relentless adversary with unwavering bravery for several years, she eventually had to say goodbye. Her passing left a profound void in the hearts of her loved ones and the communities she had touched in Portland and Draper. Shari Quai’s journey was a testament to her remarkable courage and resilience. She will always be remembered for her steadfast commitment to making a positive impact on the lives of others. Her struggle was emblematic of her spirit, and her cause of death was attributed to complications from stage IV colorectal cancer.

A Fond Farewell

Michael Linn, in a heartfelt message, announced Shari’s peaceful exit from this world on her own terms. As for her final rites and funeral arrangements, no public information is available at this time. Shari Quai’s unexpected departure has elicited an outpouring of condolences and shared grief from friends and family on various social media platforms. Her compassionate and caring nature had earned her the respect and admiration of many.

A Loss Felt by Many

Shari Quai’s departure has left a void in the lives of those who knew her. Countless individuals are mourning her loss and extending their condolences to her family and friends. We join them in expressing our deepest sympathies during this challenging time. Shari Quai will be remembered for her selflessness, her dedication to others, and the indomitable spirit with which she faced life’s challenges. Thank you for taking a moment to remember and honor Shari Quai, a remarkable individual whose legacy lives on in our hearts.

Conclusion

Shari Quai’s legacy is one of unwavering dedication and selflessness, leaving an indelible mark on the communities she touched in Portland and Draper. Her courageous battle against cancer showcased her remarkable spirit, and her passing, on April 27th, 2023, has left a void in the hearts of many. While details of her final rites remain private, the outpouring of condolences on social media is a testament to the impact she had on countless lives. Shari Quai will forever be remembered for her compassion, commitment to others, and the resilience with which she faced life’s challenges. We honor and remember this extraordinary individual whose legacy continues to inspire us all.
