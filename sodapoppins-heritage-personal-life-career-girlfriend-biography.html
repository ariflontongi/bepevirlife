Sodapoppin is an American vlogger, Twitch streamer who is famous for creating gaming content where he uses his comedic talents to make things interesting. Sodapoppin is his alias for the online community and is actually called Chance Morris. Like the famous gamer Abigale Mandler, Morris is a pretty lucky man who discovered all the right ways to make it big in the online world. This has helped him build an incredible fan base that seems to grow every day.

What ‘Sodapoppin’ does can be considered an easy job, but it’s not easy to find new material to attract hungry subscribers. People are often surprised to see the money involved in this field, but for the right people with the right ideas, you just might be able to pull it off and that is precisely what Morris is famous for. He used his creativity and humor after broadcasting himself playing World of Warcraft. His Twitch page has over 2 million subscribers with nearly 220 million views. Let us now know more about this famous internet personality.

Sodapoppin: early life and education

Chance Morris was born on February 15, 1994. The Texas-born boy had an amazing childhood, in the presence of his loving parents and 7 other siblings. Chance grew up in a wealthy family with quite wealthy parents of his. His parents were also kind and generous, and some of Chance’s siblings were adopted. Chance is the youngest child and has siblings who are much older than him.

For his education, he stayed with his parents in Austin to finish his high school and was an excellent student who had no problem securing a place for higher education. He, however, had no interest in completing college and always had this yearning to make a name for himself from gaming. So, for this very reason, he decided to drop out shortly after entering the University of Texas. Understandably, his parents weren’t happy about this, but he later proved that it was a great decision.

Sodapoppin: Race

It was in 2012 that Sodapoppin really came to life and the name was incredibly popular with its fans; he posted his first YouTube video and called it ‘Sodapoppin playing Amnesia 2’. After the first video of him, he went on to stream his gameplay for World of Warcraft, which over the years has been one of the most popular games.

Chance started gaming on a platform called ‘Xfire’ and then soon joined Justin.tv. He also put up a post on Reddit to let people know that he was switching platforms. He then started using Twitch, an Amazon subsidiary video streaming platform. It is there where he was gaining followers left, right and center.

In the summer of 2013, after great success in broadcasting, Morris moved to Michigan, to his own place. He even owns the Northern Gaming Company esports team, and earns a decent sum advertising and selling products.

sodapoppin personal life

Chance Morris is currently in a relationship with another Twitch gaming star who is more famous as Legendary Lea. However, her real name is Lea May and she is one of the most beautiful streamers in the gaming community. The couple constantly upload photos of each other on Instagram and appear to be in love.

The duo seem to be enjoying a wonderful life together and much of it is due to their common interests. They have both been successful streamers in recent years and are famous names in the gaming community. Lea and Chance were once seen together at a charity event and there Lea kissed Chance for a charity donation.

Sodapoppin: net worth and social media

According to some reliable sources, Chance Morris has a net worth ranging from $350,000 to $1 million. One of his main sources of income has always been YouTube. It is that platform that introduced him to the whole world and he has over 750k followers with over 250,000 views.

Morris is the co-owner of the Canadian esports company, ‘Northern Gaming’. However, NRG Esports paid a hefty fee to buy it out and Shaquille O’Neill now owns the company.

Sodapoppin has been a famous name on many different social platforms. He can be found in Twitch if you’re willing to watch him play. He is also active on Instagram and has over 27,000 followers, and his Youtube account of him has almost a million followers.
