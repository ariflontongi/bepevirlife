SEASON 20 of The Bachelorette officially came to a close following the romantic journey of Charity Lawson.

Fans were eager to know more about the contestants fighting for Lawson's heart.

The Bachelorette cast 2023: Who are the contestants?

On June 26, 2023, the Bachelorette Nation saw Charity Lawson embark on a journey of love with 25 contestants.

The men included:

Aaron Bryant, 29

Aaron Bryant is a software salesman from San Diego, California.

Prior to his current career, Bryant is a former football player who loves Cajun food and is a "self-proclaimed hopeless romantic."

Aaron Schwartzman, 33

Aaron Schwartzman is a firefighter who's also from San Diego, California.

According to his bio, Schwartzman loves the film Austin Powers and he enjoys being outdoors.

Adrian Hassan, 33

Adrian Hassan is a realtor from Northridge, California.

Apart from being a realtor, Hassan works as a personal trainer. Additionally, Hassan is a single father.

Caleb Arthur, 29

Caleb Arthur is a resident physician from Ann Arbor, Michigan.

When Caleb isn't working 80 hours a week, he could be found outdoors on hikes.

Caleb Balgaard, 24

Caleb Balgaard is a professional wrestler from Orlando, Florida.

Balgaard is a self-proclaimed family man with hopes of building his own in the near future.

Brayden Bowers, 24

Braden Bowers is a travel nurse from San Diego, California.

According to his bio, Bowers loves a quality cigar lounge and is hoping Charity will be the one to teach him how to salsa dance.

Dotun Olubeko, 30

Dotun Olubeko is an integrative medicine specialist from Brooklyn, New York.

Olubeko loves seeing the world — enough to take a 45-day trip throughout Europe.

He and Charity ended the season together as a couple.

James Pierce, 28

James Pierce is an attorney from Chicago, Illinois.

The Windy City native can speak a good amount of French and is a huge fan of Olivia Rodrigo.

Chris Spell, 27

Chris Spell is a Guinness World Record jumper from White Plains, New York.

Spell enjoys apple picking and binge-watching Grey's Anatomy.

Joey Graziadei, 27

Joey Graziadei is a tennis pro from Lawai, Hawaii.

When Graziadei isn't playing tennis, he is enjoying moments with his friends on the beach.

John Buresh, 27

John Buresh is a data scientist from New York, New York.

Buresh is a world traveler having visited five out of seven continents thus far.

John Henry, 30

John Henry is an underwater welder from Virginia Beach, Virginia.

John is a big ASAP Rocky fan and would love to eventually build a family with his future wife.

Joe Menzia, 32

Joe Menzia is a tech operations director from San Francisco, California.

Menzia enjoys plants, peanut M&Ms, and being adventurous.

Kaleb Kim, 26

Kaleb Kim is a construction salesman from Norcross, Georgia.

Apart from his current career, Kim dreams of one day coaching high school football.

Josh Young, 28

Josh Young is a Harvard University grad student from Bethlehem, Pennsylvania.

He currently works for a nonprofit building international affordable housing.

Khalid Hassan, 28

Khalid Hassan is a tech recruiter from Dearborn, Michigan.

Hassan enjoys hitting the gym, listening to SZA, and hopes to own a farm in the near future.

Michael Barbour, 28

Michael Barbour is a yacht captain from Chicago, Illinois.

Barbour enjoys collecting cologne and hopes to shower his future partner with sentimental gifts.

Nic Barber, 32

Nic Barber is an HR executive from Bayonne, New Jersey.

Barber hopes to relocate to Shanghai in five years' time.

Peter Cappio, 33

Peter Cappio is an airline pilot from New York, New York.

Cappio sees himself as "the life of the party" and is hoping his partner will reciprocate his energy.

Sean McLaughlin, 25

Sean McLaughlin is a software sales rep from Tampa, Florida.

McLaughlin enjoys hitting the sea and golf courses with his friends.

Spencer Storbeck, 32

Storbeck is a medical sales director from Moorpark, California.

Like Adrian, Spencer, too, is a single father. Storbeck looks up to Anthony Bourdain and dreams of getting into politics professionally.

Tanner Courtad, 30

Tanner Courtad is a mortgage lender from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.

Courtad is family oriented and has a love for dogs. In fact, Tanner and his family have rescued over 50 dogs throughout the years.

Taylor Pegg, 32

Taylor Pegg is a loan officer from Springboro, Ohio.

Pegg is a self-proclaimed goofball who spends most of his days with his miniature schnauzer, Alfred.

Warwick Reider, 27

Warwick Reider is a construction manager from Nashville, Tennessee.

Reider enjoys learning about his mom's Japanese heritage and hopes to fluently speak Japanese.

Xavier Bonner, 27

Xavier Bonner is a biomedical scientist from Carrboro, North Carolina.

Bonner once built his own computer to play video games.

Who is The Bachelorette's, Charity Lawson?

Charity Lawson is a child and family therapist born on December 31, 1995, in Columbus, Ohio.

Lawson received her master's degree in clinical mental health from Auburn University.

Prior to the new season of The Bachelorette, Lawson was on season 27 of The Bachelor featuring Zach Shallcross.

When was The Bachelorette finale?

The finale episode of season 20 of The Bachelorette premiered on Monday, August 21, 2023, on ABC at 9pm ET.

Fans can also continue to watch the show on a live-streaming service, through FuboTV, Hulu, YouTube TV, and DirecTV Stream.
