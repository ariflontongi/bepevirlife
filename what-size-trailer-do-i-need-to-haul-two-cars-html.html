What is the maximum weight capacity of a single axle trailer?

The weight capacity of a utility trailer is restricted by the number of axles it has. Axles are rated for capacities ranging from 1,000 pounds to 10,000 pounds depending on the model. It is risky to exceed the maximum weight capacity of your trailer since it might cause the axles to fail while you are driving down the road.
