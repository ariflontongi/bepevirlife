Which word has 645 meanings?

What word has over 600 definitions?

Run: 645 definitions

Though there is some debate surrounding the first place position of "run," as one of the top homographs it has (an anticipated) 645 different definitions, according to a New York Times article from 2011.

Does run 645 mean?

What are words with double meanings called?

A double entendre is a subtle literary device that uses one statement to convey two very different meanings. Taken literally, a double entendre is usually an innocent statement that has no ironic or inappropriate overtones.

What is a polysemous word?

A polysemous word is a word that has different meanings that derive from a common origin; a homograph is a word that has different meanings with unrelated origins.
